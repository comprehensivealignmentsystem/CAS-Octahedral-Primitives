prior commercial term drafts. It governs all commercial licensing of the CAS
Architecture from 2026-04-15 onward.
Governing Principle
CAS is open to learn, but licensed to deploy.

the Licensor. The tiers below are indicative. All fees are negotiable and are
specified in USD unless otherwise agreed.
