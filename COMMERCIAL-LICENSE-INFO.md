# CAS Commercial Licensing Overview

CAS (Comprehensive Alignment System) is available under a structured commercial licensing model for organisations that wish to deploy or integrate the architecture.

---

## Licensing Model

CAS commercial usage is structured into three tiers:

### 🟢 Research / Evaluation (Non-Commercial Only)
- Academic institutions
- Non-profit organisations
- Private evaluation without deployment

License: Free (non-commercial only)

---

### 🟡 Startup Tier
- Small companies and early-stage ventures
- Single product or limited deployment scope
- Internal or external commercial use within one defined system

Annual license required (pricing on request)

---

### 🔴 Enterprise Tier
- Large organisations
- Platform-level or multi-system deployment
- AI model training, architecture integration, or infrastructure-level use
- Multi-product or global deployment environments

Custom commercial agreement required

---

## Commercial Use Definition

Commercial use includes:

- Any use within a for-profit organisation
- Internal deployment within corporate systems
- Integration into products or services
- Use in AI systems, model training, or system architecture
- Redistribution or sublicensing

---

## How to Request a License

To request a commercial license, please provide:

- Description of your project
- Intended use of CAS
- Organisation size and scale
- Whether CAS will be integrated or referenced

---

## Principle

CAS is open for learning, but licensed for deployment.
