# CAS Commercial Licensing Agreement (v1.2 Summary)

## Overview

CAS (Comprehensive Alignment System) is available for commercial licensing under a structured tier model.

CAS is open to learn, but licensed to deploy.

---

## 1. Commercial Use Definition

Commercial use includes:

- Any use of CAS by a for-profit organisation
- Internal or external integration into commercial systems
- Embedding CAS into products or services
- Use in revenue-generating or operational systems
- Use in proprietary AI model design, training, or architecture

---

## 2. Licensing Tiers

### 🟡 Startup / Small Commercial
- < $5M annual revenue
- < 50 employees
- Limited to one discrete product or service line

### 🔴 Enterprise
- ≥ $5M revenue OR ≥ 50 employees
- OR multi-product/platform usage
- OR mission-critical system deployment
- OR AI model training or architecture use

---

## 3. Minimum Enterprise Terms

- Minimum annual fee: $10,000 USD
- Negotiable upward based on scope and scale
- May include exclusivity, integration support, or custom agreements

---

## 4. Audit Rights

CAS steward may, upon reasonable notice, request a non-intrusive third-party verification of compliance.

Refusal to reasonably participate may result in termination of license.

---

## 5. Transfer / Change of Control

Licenses are non-transferable without written consent.

In the event of acquisition or merger, re-negotiation is required within 90 days.

---

## 6. Contact

To request a commercial license, please provide:

- Intended use case
- Organisation details
- Scale and deployment scope
- Confirmation of product vs internal use

A tier will be assigned based on the above.
